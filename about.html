Our names are Krag Kerr and Jared Salzano, and our band is called We Do. When we're with our best friend Roland Isaac, we are called Rolando and The Wrong Turns. Our intent is to provide music for an audience whom we know is among us: the strange, curious, and light-hearted spirits who cannot get used to this dream called life.

Until recently, as far as producing art, we have always made do with what happened to be available. Using second-hand equipment and makeshift production techniques (our first two albums were recorded with iPhone Voice Recorder), we did our best to render our creative ideas into some form of media. But very recently, we have become People (we got Degrees!). In our coming of age, possibilty has begun to dawn. We can feel the expensive equipment beneath our fingertips, and at last we procure once-distant dreams of having techincal skills and enough common sense to create coherent art. To our delight, we can publish what we do, free for anybody to listen.

We are currently working on a new album. During that time, however, we may play some shows in the general Pittsburgh area. Event details will be listed on the main page, and on our Facebook page. We will also update the Jams section frequently.

Love and confetti.
